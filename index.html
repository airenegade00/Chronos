CHRONOS

Shift Planner & Salary Calculator

Last updated: February 2, 2026

Introduction

CHRONOS is a local application (local storage) for shift management, payroll calculation and time tracking. No server, no data transfer, no registration required.

DISCLAIMER

USE OF THIS APP IS AT YOUR OWN RISK.

The developer accepts no liability for: data loss, data leaks, damages from app usage, or payroll calculation inaccuracies.

Regular backups via export are recommended.

Data management

All data is stored exclusively in your browser's local storage. No data is sent to any server. Data is deleted when browser storage is cleared. Regular exports are recommended.

Salary Calculation

The payroll calculator in this app performs simplified, informational calculations only. Actual net pay may differ based on your employer's payroll system. Calculated amounts do not replace official payroll processing.

PIN Code

The PIN code is stored hashed with SHA-256. If you forget your PIN, data cannot be recovered. The PIN provides local protection only and is never sent to any server.

By using CHRONOS you agree to the above terms.
